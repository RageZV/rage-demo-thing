About Us

I Want Flowers is a boutique flower retail outlet. Founded in 2000 by Brooklyn Hughes after realising her 2 year old daughter loved flowers.

We work along side alot of botanical gardens to ensure fresh and high quality bunches, for all occasions.

I Want Flowers has a wide variety of 5 different types of flowers, ranging from Proteas, to Tulips.

We have bunches for all occasions whether it is a wedding, anniversary, graduations, or even just for a regular day where you would just like to say a simple thank you to someone.
We have what you need!

We are always looking to create an enthusiastic and family-focused environment through our flowers, events and sales.

We have 4 stores located in Sydney, Melbourne, Geelong and Brisbane.

We hope you enjoy browsing our products.
